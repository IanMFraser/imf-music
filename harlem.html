Harlem Electronics

Continuing where I left off with the Realness, Harlem Electronics further explores the behavior of chaotic oscillators but this time implementing a stochastic system for playback and performance. Around this time I was listening to a lot of Japanese noise, (mainly Pain Jerk, Incapacitants, and Hijokaiden) so I wanted the mood of this release to channel that energy as much as possible.
