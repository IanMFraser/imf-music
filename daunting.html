Keroaän - Daunting In It's Variousness:

First In A Suite Of An Indeterminate Number Of Pieces

Keroaän is a collaborative project with Reed Evan Rosenberg that has been going on for over a decade now. Our main purpose with this project is implement a musical artificial intelligence that performs with no human intervention whatsoever. "Daunting" is the result of over a year and a half of research and development. The primary synthesis method used for this was recording was Dynamic Stochastic synthesis (GENDYN).
