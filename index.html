Ian M Fraser

Ian M. Fraser is a New York based computer musician, primarily focused on algorithmic composition, generative structures, and stochastic processes. With Reed Evan Rosenberg, he works with the continual development of Keroaän, a musical artificial intelligence designed to perform without any human intervention whatsoever.
